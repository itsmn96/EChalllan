E-Challan System




E-CHALLAN SYSTEM is a web application that facilitates the traffic police and the vehicle drivers in managing the traffic fines.
The purpose is to reduce traffic violations by accurately penalizing those who break the law and reduce the task of traffic police.
The system is being implemented in order to make traffic services more accessible, simple, and transparent to the general public.
E- Challan system allows the police to issue challan for the drivers who violate rules.
The users can verify their challan if they have violated any traffic rules.
